## Project Overview

The AirPure PMF AQI Analytics Dashboard analyzes air quality data across Indian cities to identify regions with poor air quality and potential market opportunities for air purifier products.

The project combines SQL and Power BI to transform raw AQI data into actionable business insights.

## Objectives

* Analyze air quality trends across cities
* Identify cities with high pollution levels
* Evaluate pollutant concentration patterns
* Support market expansion decisions for air purifier products
* Create an interactive dashboard for decision-making

## Tools & Technologies

* SQL
* Power BI
* CSV Dataset
* Data Analysis

## Dataset

The dataset contains:

* City
* Date
* AQI (Air Quality Index)
* Pollutant Levels (PM2.5, PM10, NO2, CO, SO2, O3)
* AQI Category

## SQL Analysis

Performed:

* Data exploration
* AQI trend analysis
* City-wise pollution comparison
* Pollutant aggregation
* KPI generation

## Dashboard Features

* Average AQI KPI
* Most Polluted Cities
* AQI Trend Analysis
* Pollutant Distribution
* City-wise Comparison
* Interactive Filters and Slicers

## Key Insights

* Identified cities with consistently poor air quality
* Analyzed major pollutant contributors
* Tracked AQI changes over time
* Highlighted potential markets for air purifier products
